Describe why each writer thinks collecting is important.

1) Kameelah Janan Rasheed collects out of curiosity. She was raised to find her own answers to the questions that she had. She believes that collecting things and creating an archive is more fun than being stuck in a library.

2) Rutherford Chang collected records all of the same album. He collected them being that he was quickly intrigued by the fact of how even though they are all the same contents, they are all different. He also believes that some of these collections carry different ideas.

3) By collecting we hold onto the things that we deem most important. Something that the author believes, is that we will never know what the future value of things will be. What the author loves about the internet's archive, is that it does not have a hierarchy system of what is most important to be saved, it just save it all.
